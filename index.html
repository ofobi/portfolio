Sample GeoAI Approach

When you describe a problem here, I structure an approach that combines Remote Sensing, GIS, and ML/DL in a reproducible pipeline.

  1. Clarify the environmental or spatial question and success metrics.
  2. Identify relevant EO/GIS data sources (satellite, in‑situ, ancillary layers).
  3. Design preprocessing workflows (cloud masking, resampling, harmonization).
  4. Select ML/DL models (e.g., classifiers, segmentation networks) suited to the task.
  5. Evaluate performance with spatially aware metrics and cross‑validation.
  6. Generate maps and uncertainty estimates for decision‑makers.
